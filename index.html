Pre-Production: What do i need to have a perfect photoshoot?


  • Where and when: After paying the down payment, call me on Whats, tell me in which city you want to photograph and when and we will line up the possible day for both of you. I always post my schedule in stories and highlights.
  • Collection of References: Save on your Instagram or Pinterest, or print several photos that you identify with and that you like, whether mine or another photographer, model or friend of yours and send me everything! This will help us understand how you want to see yourself in photos!
  • Choice of looks and accessories: Based on the location and the references you sent me, I will suggest and we will choose together through Whats some looks for you to wear on the day of the photos.

What to bring to photoshoot?

  1. Pair of Glasses.
  2. Hats, Caps.
  3. Bottle of water.
